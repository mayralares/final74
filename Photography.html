Photography


Photography is a form of art that caught my attention since a young age (and by young I mean 12 years old). I enjoyed taking pretty pictures of the world around me and of my family and it is still a hobby I love doing. At times I'd play with toy cars and create a whole city with my imagination by using furniture, cups, books and anything that was available. Until I went out to explore the world more did I decide to focus on nature. The categories listed bellow are the things I enjoy taking pictures of the most.
