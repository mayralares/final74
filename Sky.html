The Sky


This page is dedicated to images I've taken of the sky. I'm really fascinated by the different colors that can come from the sky depending on the clouds and the sun. There can be a mixture of blues, blacks, greys, even pinks and purples! My favorite type of skies are the ones with yellow and orange colors, because when editting the images and playing around with the saturation level, it can create a brighter orange and even make it look red, as if the sky is on fire. These are some of the best 'sky-themed' photographs that I've taken and the majority of these are some of my favorites.
